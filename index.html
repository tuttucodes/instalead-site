// Discovery call framework

The 4-question call that closes ₹1.2L

20-minute call. You talk 30%, they talk 70%. Listen more than you speak.

01
The Situation Question
"Abhi leads kahan se aa rahe hain aapke — aur roughly per month kitne aa rahe hain?"
Let them talk. You're building a picture. Note every number they give — you'll use it later.
Follow-up: "Aur inme se kitne actually client bante hain?" → This reveals their conversion problem, not just lead problem.
02
The Desire Question
"Agar lead generation seamlessly ho jaaye — consistent, predictable — how would that change your business in the next 6 months?"
Let them paint their own dream. They're literally selling themselves on why they need your solution.
Follow-up: "Financially, roughly kitna difference hoga?" → Get a number. This anchors your ₹1.2L price against a much bigger number.
03
The Obstacle Question
"Toh yeh sab jaante hue — abhi tak kyun nahi hua? Kya roka hai?"
This surfaces their real objections BEFORE you pitch. You can address each one proactively in your presentation.
Listen for: "time nahi hai", "costly lagta hai", "pehle kisi ne kiya tha kaam nahi aaya" — each has a specific reframe ready.
04
The Close Question
"Toh agar main aaj aapko exactly dikhata hoon kaise yeh system work karega [their business] ke liye — aur numbers make sense karein — toh kya aap aage badhne ke liye ready hain?"
This is a commitment question BEFORE you show price. If they say yes, closing is natural. If no — you find out why now, not after your pitch.
After this → present the system, show the ROI math, then: "Investment ₹1.2L hai. Advance 50% aaj — hum kal start karte hain." Simple. Direct. Confident.
